Case Study · Medicare Client Demographics

Note on data privacy: Client identities are anonymized. For public hosting, replace screenshots with a synthetic or public dataset version.

Goal. Give leadership a reliable view of who our clients are and how/when they enroll to steer marketing (mailers, seminars) and staffing.

Approach. Cleaned and modeled CRM extracts in Power BI; built visuals for state distribution, demographics (age, gender, Medicaid), carrier share, application timing by month/year, election periods (AEP/OEP/SEP), and sales channel (phone vs. in‑office).

Highlights. Concentration in specific states influenced seminar locations; heavy phone enrollments shaped staffing; carrier mix informed product focus; seasonality/election-period trends guided campaign timing.

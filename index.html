Medicare Client Demographics Dashboard

Power BI Data Cleaning Visualization Insurance Domain

Leadership needed visibility into client demographics and enrollment trends to focus marketing and seminars. I aggregated CRM data, cleaned and modeled it in Power BI, and designed a dashboard covering state distribution, age, gender, Medicaid status, carrier mix, election period, and channel split (phone vs. in‑office).

  • Geography: state-by-state distribution with top states highlighted.
  • Demographics: age histogram; gender share; Medicaid segmentation.
  • Business: carrier mix; application timing by month/year and election period.

AI R&D Spending & Impact

Tableau Visualization Visualization

Analyzed synthetic financial data to link AI R&D spending, revenue growth, and stock performance. Built a Tableau dashboard with trend lines, efficiency ratios, and event annotations to show how product launches and investment patterns align with market impacts.

  • R&D & Revenue: Trend lines comparing AI research spending to revenue growth.
  • Market impact: Stock performance over time with event markets for product launches.
  • Efficiency Ratios: Visualization of revenue generated per dollar of R&D investment.
